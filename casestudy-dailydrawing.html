The Daily Drawing Project

The Daily Drawing Project was the start of my journey to graphic design and illustration. On January 1st, 2016 I made a new year's resolution to complete one illustration every single day for an entire year. I was unhappy with my work and career and wanted to find a way to incorporate creativity into my everyday life. While I was still creating art at that time, I was having trouble completing projects and found I had little motivation. The objective of this resolution was to start and finish an illustration everyday. I chose a sketchbook with small dimensions and decided that I would stick with pen and ink as my tools, in an effort to keep the project as simple as possible.

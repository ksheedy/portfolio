Oh, Hello

My hands have marker on them. My clothes have paint stains. My ring finger has a callus from pressing too hard with my pen. Creating and drawing is how I learn about and best communicate with the world. Or maybe sarcasm? Sarcasm is a close second.

I love design because it allows us to connect and share ideas with others. It allows us to form connections and communicate without saying a single awkward word. Whether it is illustration, graphic design, motion, print or web, I would love to work with you and create something together (and that is definitely not sarcasm, I swear).

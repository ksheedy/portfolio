Craft Commons

Craft Commons is a speciality craft and art shop. This business focuses on providing artists and crafters with all of the tools of their trade, as well as offering in-store workshops. Craft Commons’ brand highlights the handmade nature of their business by employing a hand-drawn style. The objective of this project was to demonstrate practical applications for illustration in branding.

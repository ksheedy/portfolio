Package Design

Olivia Burton creates intricate and delicate watch and jewelry designs. The brief for this project was to redesign the existing Olivia Burton watch packaging design so that it was completely reusable and recyclable. The bee features prominently across Olivia Burton's branding and is the inspiration for this packaging.
